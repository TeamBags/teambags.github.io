Timely purchases in the right volume with the new solution from Acurella

The ability to choose the optimal volume of the order batch is a prerequisite for improving the efficiency of the enterprise. If the batch is too large, it can be fraught with freezing of funds. In addition, if it is too small, administrative costs increase for repeated orders.

Supply chain management (SCM) software helps to integrate the management of raw materials, materials, products and services related to production and logistics. Thus, the customer always receives the ordered goods on time and in the right amount, and the company reduces costs significantly.

Representatives of different companies that are involved in the search for materials, the manufacturing process of products and their delivery can use the supply chain management system. At the same time, each of them will have limited access to the processes where it participates.

Acurella system can simplify at times the interaction:

  • of merchandise;
  • financiers;
  • employees of the logistics service;
  • managers;
  • administrators;
  • couriers;
  • top managers.

Acurella software helps to automate the management of all stages of supply of the enterprise and control the movement of goods: the purchase of raw materials, production, and distribution of products.
